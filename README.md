## Project Overview

Analyzed 9,994 retail transactions to uncover revenue drivers, profitability trends, regional performance gaps, and discount-related profit leakage. Built interactive dashboards and generated business-focused insights to support data-driven decision-making.

This project demonstrates how raw transactional data can be transformed into meaningful business intelligence through SQL analysis, Power BI reporting, and interactive dashboard development.

The primary objective was to identify:

* High-performing and low-performing business segments
* Revenue and profitability trends across categories
* Regional performance differences

## Key Business Insights

### Category Analysis

* Technology generated the highest profitability and delivered the strongest margins.
* Office Supplies maintained stable and consistent business performance.
* Furniture generated substantial revenue but significantly lower profitability due to aggressive discounting.

### Product Analysis

* Copiers emerged as the most profitable product segment.
* Phones consistently contributed strong revenue and profit.
* Tables generated significant losses despite healthy sales volume.
* Bookcases showed weak profitability performance.

### Regional Analysis

* West region achieved the highest profit margin.
* East region maintained strong overall performance.
* Central region recorded the lowest profitability, indicating improvement opportunities.

### Discount Impact

* Higher discount levels consistently reduced profit margins.
* Loss-making products were concentrated within heavily discounted categories.
* Pricing optimization presents a significant opportunity for profitability improvement.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Reassess discount strategies for loss-making product categories.
* Focus investment on high-margin product segments.
* Replicate successful regional sales strategies across lower-performing regions.
* Improve seasonal inventory planning based on recurring Q4 demand patterns.
* Implement KPI monitoring to track revenue and profitability performance.
